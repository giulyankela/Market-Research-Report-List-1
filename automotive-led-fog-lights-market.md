<p><p>The automotive LED fog lights market is primarily divided into front fog lights and rear fog lights. Front fog lights are designed to illuminate the road directly in front of the vehicle, reducing glare and improving visibility during fog, rain, or snow. In contrast, rear fog lights enhance the visibility of the vehicle from behind, helping to alert drivers in low-visibility conditions. Both types enhance safety and are increasingly favored due to their energy efficiency and longer lifespan compared to traditional halogen lights.</p></p>
