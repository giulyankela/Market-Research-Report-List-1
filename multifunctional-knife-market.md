<p><p>The multifunctional knife market is categorized into three main types: single edge, double edge, and multi-blade knives. Single edge knives feature one sharp side, ideal for tasks requiring precision, like slicing. Double edge knives have sharpness on both sides, making them versatile for cutting and thrusting actions. Multi-blade knives combine several tools, such as blades, screwdrivers, and scissors, into one compact design, appealing to users seeking convenience and versatility for various outdoor and everyday tasks.</p></p>
