<p><strong>The Full body CT Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CT Scanners</li><li>PET-CT Scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The full body CT market comprises two primary types: CT scanners and PET-CT scanners. CT scanners utilize X-ray technology to create detailed cross-sectional images of the body, aiding in the diagnosis of various conditions. In contrast, PET-CT scanners combine positron emission tomography (PET) with computed tomography (CT), offering metabolic and anatomical insights simultaneously. This fusion enhances accuracy in detecting cancers and evaluating treatment responses, making both scanner types essential tools in modern medical imaging and diagnostics.</p></p>
