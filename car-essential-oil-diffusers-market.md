<p><p>The car essential oil diffuser market includes three main types: Ultrasonic Car Diffusers, Electric Heat Car Diffusers, and Evaporated Car Diffusers. Ultrasonic diffusers use vibrations to disperse essential oils into fine mist, providing a gentle scent. Electric heat diffusers warm the oils to release fragrance, offering a more intense aroma. Evaporated diffusers utilize airflow to naturally disperse the essential oils, creating a subtle and steady scent. Each type caters to different preferences for aroma intensity and technology.</p></p>
