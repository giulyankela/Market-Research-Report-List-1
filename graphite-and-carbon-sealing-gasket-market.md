<p><p>The graphite and carbon sealing gasket market is segmented into two primary types: pure flexible graphite gaskets and pure flexible graphite-metal inserted gaskets. Pure flexible graphite gaskets are made entirely from high-purity graphite, offering excellent thermal and chemical resistance, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, pure flexible graphite-metal inserted gaskets incorporate a metal core, enhancing mechanical strength and stability under extreme conditions. Both types are widely used in industries like oil and gas, power generation, and chemical processing.</p></p>
