<p><ul><li>Flywheel Front Ellipticals</li><li>Flywheel Rear Ellipticals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial elliptical market is characterized by two main types: flywheel front ellipticals and flywheel rear ellipticals. Flywheel front ellipticals feature a flywheel located at the front, offering a more natural and forward-positioned stride, often favored for their smooth motion and space-efficient design. In contrast, flywheel rear ellipticals have the flywheel positioned at the back, providing a more traditional elliptical motion that typically engages users' lower body muscles more effectively. Both types cater to different user preferences and workout intensities.</p></p>
