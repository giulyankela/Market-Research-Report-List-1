<p><p>The disposable plastic blood bags market includes several types based on the number of compartments. Triple bags further facilitate separating platelets, enhancing blood components' utilization. Quadruple bags provide the most versatile options, enabling the collection of multiple components such as whole blood, platelets, plasma, and red blood cells, catering to diverse clinical needs and improving transfusion efficiency.</p></p>
