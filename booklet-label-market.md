<p><p>The booklet label market is categorized into three main types: pressure sensitive labels, glue-applied labels, and others. Pressure sensitive labels are adhesive-backed and can be applied without heat or water, making them versatile and cost-effective. Glue-applied labels utilize liquid adhesives to bind directly to surfaces, offering strong adhesion and durability. The "others" category includes specialty labels, such as those requiring unique application methods or materials, catering to niche markets with specific labeling needs.</p></p>
