<p><strong>The Optical Semiconductor Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dark Field Inspection</li><li>Bright Field Inspection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical semiconductor inspection equipment is categorized into Dark Field and Bright Field inspection types, each serving distinct purposes in semiconductor manufacturing. Dark Field inspection utilizes scattered light to detect defects on surfaces, emphasizing small irregularities by illuminating the sample at an oblique angle. In contrast, Bright Field inspection captures reflected light from the surface, providing a direct view of the sample and identifying larger defects or variations in reflectivity. Both methods are crucial for ensuring high-quality semiconductor production.</p></p>
