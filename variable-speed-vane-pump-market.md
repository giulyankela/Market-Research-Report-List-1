<p><strong>The Variable Speed Vane Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unbalanced Vane Pump</li><li>Balanced Vane Pump</li><li>Variable Displacement Vane Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The variable speed vane pump market includes three main types: unbalanced vane pumps, balanced vane pumps, and variable displacement vane pumps. Unbalanced vane pumps utilize a design where vanes are not uniformly distributed, resulting in higher pressures but lower efficiency. Balanced vane pumps have an even distribution of vanes, which provides improved efficiency and reduced wear. Variable displacement vane pumps change their displacement based on system demands, allowing for better control and energy savings. Together, these pumps cater to diverse applications in fluid handling.</p></p>
