<p><ul><li>Integral Heat Treatment</li><li>Surface Heat Treatment</li><li>Chemical Heat Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat treating market comprises three main types: integral heat treatment, surface heat treatment, and chemical heat treatment. Integral heat treatment involves the uniform heating and cooling of an entire component to enhance its overall mechanical properties. Surface heat treatment targets only the exterior layers, improving hardness and wear resistance while preserving core toughness. Chemical heat treatment alters the material's surface through processes like carburizing or nitriding, enhancing surface properties and corrosion resistance. Each type serves specific industrial applications, optimizing performance and durability.</p></p>
