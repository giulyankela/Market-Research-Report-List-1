<p><p>The car air conditioner filter market primarily features two types: single effect filters and double effect filters. Single effect filters focus on basic air purification, capturing dust and particles to improve air quality within the vehicle. As consumer awareness about air quality rises, the demand for both filter types is growing, catering to different preferences for air conditioning efficiency and comfort.</p></p>
