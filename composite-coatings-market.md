<p><p>The composite coatings market consists of several key types, including Laser Melt Injection, Brazing, Electroless Plating, and others. Laser Melt Injection involves using high-energy lasers to fuse coatings onto substrates for enhanced durability. Brazing is a process that joins metals through melting a filler material, creating strong bonds. Electroless Plating is a chemical process that deposits metal layers without the need for electricity, ensuring uniform coverage. Other methods may include thermal spraying and chemical vapor deposition, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
