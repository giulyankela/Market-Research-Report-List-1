<p><p>The HPLC detectors market includes various types tailored for specific analytical needs. UV-Visible detectors measure absorbance at specific wavelengths, making them ideal for detecting organic compounds. Refractive Index detectors measure changes in refractive index, suitable for substances without strong UV absorbance. Evaporative Light Scattering Detectors (ELSD) are useful for non-volatile compounds. Fluorescence detectors are highly sensitive, detecting compounds that fluoresce. Other detectors encompass diverse technologies like mass spectrometry and conductivity, catering to niche applications and enhancing analytical flexibility.</p></p>
