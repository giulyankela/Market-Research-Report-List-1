<p><p>The large cooling fan market, specifically fans above 200mm, is primarily divided into two types: centrifugal fans and axial fans. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to increase air pressure and are ideal for high resistance applications, effectively moving air in various industrial settings. In contrast, axial fans draw air parallel to the fan's axis, providing high airflow rates with lower pressure increases, making them suitable for cooling and ventilation in residential, commercial, and industrial environments.</p></p>
